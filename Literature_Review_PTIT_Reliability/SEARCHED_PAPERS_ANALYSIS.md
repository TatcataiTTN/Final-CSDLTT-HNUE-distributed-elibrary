## 2. High-Priority Papers (Must Include)

### A. Foundational/High-Citation Papers

#### 1. Chen et al. (2021) - Nature
- **Title**: "An integrated space-to-ground quantum communication network over 4,600 kilometres"
- **Journal**: Nature 589, 214-219
- **Citations**: >1000
- **DOI**: 10.1038/s41586-020-03093-8
- **Key Contribution**: First integrated satellite-fiber QKD network, 150 users, 32 trusted relays
- **Relevance**: Benchmark for practical satellite QKD deployment

#### 2. Liao et al. (2017) - Nature
- **Title**: "Satellite-to-ground quantum key distribution"
- **Journal**: Nature 549, 43-47
- **Citations**: >2500
- **DOI**: 10.1038/nature23655
- **Key Contribution**: First Micius satellite QKD demonstration, decoy-state BB84
- **Relevance**: Foundation for all satellite QKD research

#### 3. Yin et al. (2017) - Science
- **Title**: "Satellite-based entanglement distribution over 1200 kilometers"
- **Journal**: Science 356, 1140-1144
- **Citations**: >2000
- **DOI**: 10.1126/science.aan3211
- **Key Contribution**: Record-breaking entanglement distribution
- **Relevance**: Demonstrates long-distance quantum correlation preservation

#### 4. Pirandola et al. (2020) - Advances in Optics and Photonics
- **Title**: "Advances in quantum cryptography"
- **Journal**: Advances in Optics and Photonics 12(4), 1012-1236
- **Citations**: >1500
- **DOI**: 10.1364/AOP.361502
- **Key Contribution**: Comprehensive 225-page review of QKD advances
- **Relevance**: Essential reference for all aspects of QKD

#### 5. Scarani et al. (2009) - Reviews of Modern Physics
- **Title**: "The security of practical quantum key distribution"
- **Journal**: Reviews of Modern Physics 81(3), 1301-1350
- **Citations**: >4000

- **Key Contribution**: Definitive security analysis framework
- **Relevance**: Foundation for security proofs
